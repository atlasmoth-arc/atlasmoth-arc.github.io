Mental Healing Hands

Serving communities affected by conflict in Karenni State, this retreat center provides a safe environment for psychological healing. The architecture utilizes a familiar vernacular typology, locally sourced timber, and bamboo louvers to blend into the forest. Spatial programming includes sensory gardens, meditation pavilions, and a healing pond to encourage community bonding and mental restoration.
